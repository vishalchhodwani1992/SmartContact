About Smart Contacts

Smart Contact is made to manage your contacts wisely.

VIP Contacts are those contacts that you don't want to miss while your phone is on silent mode or in vibration mode.

SmartContacts will bring your phone in ringing mode while VIP contact calling you and after completion of call it will set again on previous mode(silent or vibrate).

You can enable and disable the VIP Contacts anytime by clicking on menu.

Block Contacts has the list of numbers whose call you don't want attend,
Smart Contacts will reject automatic the Block Listed calls.
